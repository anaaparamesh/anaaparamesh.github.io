Requirements

Middle School Requirements

6th grade in the Littleton Middle School are required to perform one hour of community service, seventh grade to perform two, and eight grade to perform three hours.

National Junior Honor Society Requirements

In order to apply for the National Junior Honor Society, you must be in the seventh or eighth grade, and must have done twleve hours of community service
